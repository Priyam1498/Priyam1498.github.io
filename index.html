About Me

Hello! I'm Priyam Srivastava, currently pursuing my PhD in Information Sciences at the University of Pittsburgh. My academic journey has led me to the intersection of Quantum Machine Learning and Quantum Technologies, where I am deeply passionate about developing cutting-edge solutions for quantum sensing and network technologies.

I have a solid foundation in Physics, having completed my Master's degree at the Indian Institute of Science Education and Research (IISER), Bhopal, where I developed skills in both experimental and theoretical quantum technologies. My current research focuses on Variational Quantum Algorithms and their applications in Quantum Sensing and Quantum Networks.

Beyond academia, I love to read and immerse myself in different worlds through books. I'm also an avid anime fan and enjoy watching shows that explore complex themes and characters. Whenever I get the chance, I like to go out on treks, exploring the natural beauty of new places, which refreshes and inspires me.
